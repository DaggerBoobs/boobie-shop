  • Contribute: Every dabloob spent in the Boobie Shop — whether on Chaos Effects, IRL Dares, or Digital Rewards — counts toward the current Community Goals.
  • Unlock: Once a goal bar hits 100%, a Community Raffle is triggered for that prize.
  • Enter: Once unlocked, a command is posted in chat — every viewer gets one free entry.
  • Boost: Spend dabloobs for bonus entries to increase your chances (Max 3 per person).
